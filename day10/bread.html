Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients
  2. Cover mixing bowl with wet towel
  3. Let it sit in the fridge for 3 hours
  4. Kneed dough
  5. Bake in oven for 45 minutes at 300°F
